Fascinating Stationery Facts

The First Pencil

The first mass-produced pencils were made in Germany in 1662. Before that, pencils were handmade and quite expensive.

Post-it Notes Discovery

Post-it Notes were invented by accident when Dr. Spencer Silver created a weak adhesive and Art Fry used it to mark pages in his hymnal.

Pen Ink Colors

Blue ink is the most common color for pens because it's easier to read than black and looks more professional than other colors.

Paper Sizes
